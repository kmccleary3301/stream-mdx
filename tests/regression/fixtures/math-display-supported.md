# Math Display Supported

Supported display math should anticipate conservatively and still keep trailing prose visible.

$$
\frac{a}{b} + \sqrt{x}
$$

- List item with display math:

  $$
  z_i^2 + \frac{1}{n}
  $$

  trailing prose must remain visible after the display block.

> Blockquote with display math:
>
> $$
> \sqrt{y} + x_i^2
> $$
>
> trailing prose must remain visible after the quoted display math.

Final paragraph after supported display math content.
